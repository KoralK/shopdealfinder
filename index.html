How to Use the App

1. Search for Deals

  • Enter Your Search Query: In the search box, type in what you're looking for. It could be a specific product, a brand, or a type of deal.
  • Press the Search Icon: Tap the magnifying glass icon to start your search.

2. View Results

  • The app will show you a list of deals related to your search. Each deal includes a title and a link.
  • Tap on a Deal: If a deal catches your eye, just tap on it to view more details and visit the store's website.
